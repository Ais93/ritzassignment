The Ritz London

Set in the bustling Mayfair and St. James district, this luxe, iconic 1906 hotel is a 4-minute walk from the Royal Academy of Arts and a 7-minute walk from Piccadilly Circus. The ornate rooms with Louis XVI-style decor and antique furnishings have free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, iPod docks, and minibars. Suites add living/dining areas, grand pianos, butlers and an airport shuttle in the hotel's Rolls Royce. A palatial restaurant offers high-end dining and while afternoon tea is served in a palm tree-lined courtyard. An elegant art deco bar serves bespoke cocktails. There's also a posh salon offering spa treatments and event space.
